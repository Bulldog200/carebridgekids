关于康桥儿童健康 | About CareBridge Kids

康桥儿童健康是一个提供中英文双语内容的儿童健康信息平台，致力于帮助家庭便捷获取清晰、可靠的儿童健康知识，并直连美国疾控中心（CDC）及各地卫生部门等官方信息来源。

CareBridge Kids is a bilingual pediatric health information platform providing families with clear, trusted resources in English and Simplified Chinese, with links to official sources such as the CDC and local health departments.
