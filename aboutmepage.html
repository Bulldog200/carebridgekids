我们的使命

许多家庭在获取关键儿童健康信息时，面临语言和文化的隔阂。康桥儿童健康正是为弥合这一鸿沟而创立。我们是一个中英文双语儿童健康信息平台，致力于帮助家长与照护者——尤其是移民及多语言家庭——清晰、便捷地获取源自权威机构的健康知识。我们系统地整合并链接来自美国疾病控制与预防中心(CDC)、美国儿科学会(AAP)及纽约市/州卫生部门等官方信源的信息。 康桥儿童健康是一个独立的教育项目，旨在提供可靠信息，不能替代专业医生的诊断与建议。 清晰沟通，科学养育。

Our Mission

Many families face language barriers when accessing pediatric health information. CareBridge Kids was created to help bridge that gap. CareBridge Kids is a bilingual pediatric health information platform that supports parents and caregivers—especially immigrant and multilingual families—by providing clear, reliable, and trusted children’s health resources in both English and Simplified Chinese. The website organizes essential pediatric health information with direct links to official sources, including the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP), and New York City and New York State health departments. CareBridge Kids is an independent educational project and does not replace professional medical advice.
