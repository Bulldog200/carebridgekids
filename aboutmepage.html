我们的使命

康桥儿童健康 是一个双语儿科健康信息平台，旨在帮助家庭——特别是移民家庭——获取清晰、可靠且权威的儿童健康资源。网站提供英语和简体中文的儿科健康信息，并直接链接至美国疾病控制与预防中心（CDC）、美国儿科学会（AAP）以及纽约州和纽约市卫生部门等官方来源。康桥儿童健康 对公开的健康信息进行整理与组织，以提升信息的可及性与理解度。本网站不修改官方内容，仅用于教育目的，不能替代专业的医疗建议、诊断或治疗。

Our Mission

CareBridge Kids is a bilingual pediatric health information platform designed to help families—especially immigrant families—access clear, reliable, and authoritative health resources for children. The website provides pediatric health information in both English and Simplified Chinese, with direct links to official sources such as the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP), and New York State and City health departments. CareBridge Kids curates and organizes publicly available health information to improve accessibility and understanding. The website does not modify official content and is intended for educational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
