Our Mission

CareBridge Kids is a bilingual pediatric health information platform designed to help families access clear, reliable, and trusted health resources for children. The website provides essential pediatric health information in both English and Simplified Chinese, with direct links to official sources such as the CDC and local health departments.

CareBridge Kids aims to support parents and caregivers—especially immigrant families—by improving access to accurate health information while maintaining clarity, safety, and educational integrity.

This website is for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice.

我们的使命

康桥儿童健康是一个中英文双语的儿童健康信息平台，致力于帮助家庭更便捷地获取 清晰、可靠、权威的儿童健康资源。网站提供英文与简体中文对照的儿童健康信息， 并链接至美国 CDC 及当地卫生部门等官方来源。

康桥儿童健康旨在支持家长与照护者，特别是移民家庭，提高获取准确儿童健康信息的可及性。

本网站仅用于健康教育，不可替代专业医疗建议。
